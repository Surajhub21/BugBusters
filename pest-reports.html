Real-Time Pest Reports

Stay informed about pest activity in your area

Cockroach Activity

High Risk
Increased cockroach activity reported in residential areas. Warm weather conditions are contributing to higher infestation rates.

Prevention Tips:

  • Keep food in sealed containers
  • Clean up spills immediately
  • Fix leaky pipes and faucets
  • Seal cracks and crevices

Rodent Activity

Medium Risk
Moderate rodent activity detected. Recent weather changes have increased nesting behavior in urban areas.

Prevention Tips:

  • Store garbage in sealed containers
  • Trim vegetation near buildings
  • Seal entry points around pipes
  • Keep pet food indoors

Ant Activity

Low Risk
Normal ant activity levels reported. Regular monitoring recommended during summer months.

Prevention Tips:

  • Clean up food crumbs
  • Store sweet items in containers
  • Seal entry points
  • Maintain clean surfaces
